## Campaign 1: Customer Notifications

### Basic Information

**Campaign Name (Internal Use Only):**
```
Cosmic Fit Club - Customer Notifications
```

**Campaign Description:**
```
Cosmic Fit Club sends account notifications to customers regarding their fitness class bookings and memberships. Messages include registration confirmations, class reminders, schedule changes, cancellation notifications, and important studio updates. All recipients explicitly opt-in through a checkbox during account registration or via our SMS preferences page at cosmicfitclub.com/sms/opt-in where they submit their phone number and agree to receive notifications. Messages are transactional and directly related to the customer's booked services or active membership.
```

**Use Case Selection:**
```

```

**Sub-Use Case (if applicable):**
```
Select: "Customer Care"
```

### Opt-In Details

**Opt-In Workflow Description:**
```
Customers opt-in through two methods: 1) During account registration, they check a box stating "I agree to receive SMS notifications about my classes and bookings" which includes a link to our SMS terms. 2) Existing customers can visit cosmicfitclub.com/sms/opt-in where they enter their phone number and check "I agree to receive SMS messages" before clicking submit. The opt-in timestamp and phone number are stored in our database. All messages include opt-out instructions. Users can opt-out anytime by replying STOP or visiting their account preferences.
```

```
Select: "Verbal or Written Consent"
```

## Important Notes for Submission

### Before You Submit:

1. ✅ Make sure your Brand registration is complete and approved first
2. ✅ Have your business EIN/Tax ID ready
3. ✅ Verify your website (cosmicfitclub.com) is live and has privacy policy
4. ✅ Take screenshots of your opt-in page (/sms/opt-in)
5. ✅ Double-check all sample messages include "Reply STOP to opt out"

### After Submission:

- **Approval typically takes 1-2 weeks**
- You'll receive email notifications about approval status
- Once approved, your messages will have better deliverability
- Keep your actual messages similar to the approved samples

### Tips for Approval:

- Be honest about volumes (start low, you can increase later)
- Keep sample messages professional and generic
- Don't mention specific prices, names, or emails
- Make opt-in process crystal clear
- Answer all questions completely

### If Rejected:

Common rejection reasons and fixes:
- "Unclear opt-in" → Add more detail about when/where users opt in
- "High risk content" → Remove any financial details or personal info from samples
- "Volume too high" → Lower your estimated volume
- "Wrong use case" → Make sure use case matches your actual messaging
